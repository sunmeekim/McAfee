TV

Sit back and relax – you’ve got the
ultimate protection for your smart TV

McAfee® Security for TV

Help keep your Samsung TV† secure against viruses and threats. You can remove anything suspicious after scanning your apps on TV.
No extra costs.

HOW TO ACCESS McAfee® Security for TV

  • 1. Press ‘Smart hub’ button on your remote control and press APPS
  • 2. Find McAfee Security for TV on APPS panel
    ① Use search bar in the upper right corner of TV screen
    ② Press a McAfee promotion banner
  • 3. Press Open button
  • 4. Press Get started
  • 5. Press Scan My TV regularly to protect your TV from viruses and threats

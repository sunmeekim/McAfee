About this website

Protecting every connected device in your home is absolutely essential today as it puts entire home network at risk if one device is attacked. As McAfee is aware of consumer fears that underscores the need for robust cross-device security, our protection is designed to address growing concerns like these across multiple devices.

McAfee, one of the world’s leading independent cybersecurity companies, has collaborated with Samsung, one of world's leading electronics companies to provide pre-installed security software protection on Samsung Smart TVs, Samsung PCs, and the Samsung mobile devices worldwide.†

This website is built for our customers in USA and Korea to learn more about McAfee security solutions pre-installed on their Samsung devices as well as exclusive offers. Additional countries to launch this website will be added throughout the year.
